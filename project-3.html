SmartSpend AI – Your Intelligent Financial Companion

Project Overview

Managing finances can be overwhelming—but what if you had an assistant that not only tracked your spending but also predicted your future expenses? SmartSpend AI is an intelligent financial tracking system that does more than just record expenses. Powered by AI, it analyzes your past transactions to forecast upcoming spending patterns and helps you make smarter financial decisions. With a clean and modern interface, it provides insightful visualizations to help you understand where your money goes. From daily budgeting to long-term planning, SmartSpend AI turns your financial data into clear, actionable insights—making money management simpler and smarter. Track → Predict → Visualize → Improve
